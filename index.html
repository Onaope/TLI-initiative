Welcome to the Live initiative

ABOUT US

When the Live Initiative was created in 2019, it was created out of sheer willingness and determination of a young and brilliant Nigerian lady whose aggravated frustrations of what is now know and has been accepted as the norm inspired what she believes will significantly redirect the purpose, revamp the lives and change the realities of underprivileged Nigerians. Today, herself and her team are intensively trying to overlap the lines and gaps between abilities, capabilities and opportunities among the rich and the poor in the Nigerian society.
This is The Live Initiative.

MISSION AND VISION STATEMENT

OUR MISSION.
We are on a RESCUE mission.
OUR VISION.
Our vision is to make great Heroes and Heroines out of every underprivileged Nigerian.

OUR PROGRAMS

WORK TO EARN
The Work2Earn programme by The Live Initiative is targeted at deliberately improving, relentlessly empowering and enabling indigent Nigerian youths eclipse their impending financial challenges by adorning them with in-demand 21st century skills allowing them to not only be able to chase their dreams and scale their paths out of poverty but also add value to the society and stay in school while doing so.

CORE VALUES

  • Empowerment: We achieve our goals by Empowering Nigerian youths from low income communities.
  • Entrepreneurship: Inculcating the entrepreneurial spirit into every Nigerian youth in order to create a financially balanced society.
  • Leadership: Grooming leaders from low income communities.
  • Skills: Upskilling Nigerian youths with in-demand 21st century skills.
  • Youth: We believe the youths are the Leaders of the next generation and we are earnestly preparing

TARGET AUDIENCE

  • Indigent youths between the age of 13-19
  • Widowed,single and divorced mothers
  • Prisoners almost completing their jail term
  • Students from public schools and low cost private schools.
  • Youths from low income communities.
